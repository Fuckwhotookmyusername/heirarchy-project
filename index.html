Welcome

This site explores a big question:

Why hasn’t anything stopped hierarchy in government and the economy, and how has it lasted so long?

🔍 Introduction

  • Hierarchy Defined: A system where people or groups are ranked one above the other based on power or status.
  • Where It's Found: Hierarchies are everywhere—from governments to global corporations.
  • Purpose of This Site: To explore how these systems started, why they last, and what we can do about them.

🌍 Why It Matters

  • Influence on Society: Hierarchical systems affect laws, rights, wages, opportunities, and who gets heard.
  • Democracy vs. Hierarchy: Even in democracies, power often flows from the top down.
  • Everyday Impact: From job titles to voting systems, hierarchy shapes our lives more than most realize.
