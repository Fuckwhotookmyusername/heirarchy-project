Understanding Hierarchy in Our Government and Economy

Exploring the Layers of Power

In this project, we will break down the hierarchy of our government, major corporations, and discuss potential solutions to improve the balance of power.

Facts about Government, Corporations, and More

  • From local governments to federal systems, power is distributed across multiple layers. This allows for efficient governance but can also lead to inefficiencies and lack of accountability.

  • The President (or Prime Minister) has control over key decisions such as military actions, economic policy, and foreign relations. However, checks and balances limit their power.

  • CEOs of major corporations make strategic decisions that shape entire industries. Their influence extends beyond the company, impacting global markets and employment.
