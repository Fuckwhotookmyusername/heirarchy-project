Corporate Hierarchy: Unveiling the Power Structure

The Hidden Layers Behind Power

Power isn't always as visible as we think. From governments to corporations, there are hidden structures shaping the way we live. Let’s uncover how these hierarchies shape our world.

Did You Know?

  • Government hierarchies exist at local, state, and federal levels. Each tier has specific roles, from creating laws to enforcing them. These layers can both support and slow down decision-making in large populations.

  • Top corporations like Apple and Amazon have greater wealth than some countries. Their executives control vast resources, affect labor markets, and shape consumer behavior on a global scale.

  • CEOs earn hundreds of times more than average employees. With lobbying power and insider influence, hierarchies often protect the wealthy and limit opportunities for the majority.
